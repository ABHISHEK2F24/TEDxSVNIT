Nikhil Malhotra

Renowned for being an amazing father, Nikhil Malhotra was instrumental in the innovation of the healthcare robot Saarthi. His inputs, along with his little son’s, helped the healthcare robot become a reality which has the potential to help millions, maybe even billions of people all over the world. The initial idea of Saarthi was achieved by Malhotra through the numerous questions asked by his four-year old son, Angad, about what a robot could do in future. Currently based in kalyan, Mumbai

Surmai Kaushik

Surmai Kaushik is a CSR Executive with Mahindra Susten. All it took to get Surmai Kaushik’s heart pounding was a phone call inviting her to be a part of Robert Swan’s Mission 2041. Robert Swan’s Mission 2041 is all about protecting Antarctica by cleaning up 326 million tonnes of CO2 before the 2025. On her journey, Surmai observed some detrimental effects of global warming – melting glaciers, leading to loss of untouched fresh water. Dwelling in silence there, Surmai introspected how humans were changing the last white wilderness landscape and decided to change these things and became a climate advocate.
