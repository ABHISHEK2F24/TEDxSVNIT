TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 30 years ago, TED has grown to support its mission with multiple initiatives. The two annual TED Conferences invite the world’s leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes or less. Many of these talks are then made available, free, at TED.com. TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Jane Goodall, Elizabeth Gilbert, Sir Richard Branson, Nandan Nilekani, Philippe Starck, Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, Sal Khan and Daniel Kahneman.

The annual TED Conference takes place each spring in Vancouver, British Columbia, along with the TEDActive simulcast event in nearby Whistler. TED has established the annual TED Prize, where exceptional individuals with a wish to change the world get help translating their wishes into action; TEDx, which supports individuals or groups in hosting local, self- organized TED-style events around the world, and the TED Fellows program, helping world-changing innovators from around the globe to amplify the impact of their remarkable projects and activities.

About TEDx, x = independently organized event

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event) The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized. TEDxSVNIT

TEDxSVNIT is an independently organised TEDx event operated under license from TED. Innovation takes place when diverse ideas reach out to the inquisitive minds of people. At TEDxSVNIT, we present an opportunity to bring up out of the box thinkers and let their ideas, innovations and inventions kindle a think tank among the student. With an event such as this, we aim to create a turbulence in the mainstream engineering mindset of students so that newer ideas incubate with a blend of art and technology.

TEDxSVNIT shares the same passion as TED. We are here to spread your ideas all across the globe. Our objective is to reach out and inspire people with “Ideas Worth Sharing” which has the potential to help people make a strong difference in their lives.

TEDxSVNIT is oriented at developing a community of like minded highly informed and engaged innovators, designers, thinkers, technologists and environmentally and socially concerned individuals in an institute of national importance - SVNIT.

FAQ's

Who can participate?

Anyone who wants to get influenced and make a positive change in their life and the lives of others is most welcome.

Can I attend event without registration?

No, you must fill the registration form if you want to attend the event.

Why do I need to register?

TEDxSVNIT is a niche event only meant for the most dedicated and ambitious people of this generation and therefore there is a cap on the number of people being invited to our event.

How does the application process work?

First, you must submit the registration form. We have an evaluation panel which consists of our team members as well outside people who will assess you based on your answers. Once the evaluation is completed and you meet their expectations, we will inform you via email for the next process.

What do I do after submitting the registration form?

All you have to do is wait for a confirmation email that we will send to your registered email after your registration form has been evaluated. Patience is the key!

What is the registration fee?

The registration fee for attending the event is Rs. 1000. You will be asked to pay the amount only after you are successfully registered for the event.

How do I pay the registration fee?

Various payment gateways will be made available to all the successful registrants.

What am I expected to bring?

Just bring your heart and curiosity to the event. Oh and don't forget to carry your confirmation ticket! Rest of the things like Registration Kit, note taking material, cool schwags, lunch selfie booths (to show off) etc. will be taken care by us.

Did we miss something?

Please email us at contact@tedxsvnit.com. Also don't forget to follow our social media platforms for the latest updates.
